What is a Building Management System?

A Building Management System (BMS) is a centralized, computer-based platform that monitors, controls, and optimizes a building's mechanical and electrical systems.

It enables real-time management of refrigeration,HVAC , lighting, power, fire safety, and security systems, ensuring efficient, reliable, and sustainable building operation.

Complete Hardware & Software Integration

A BMS integrates both hardware and software, allowing users to retrieve data from equipment and regulate it from a single control point.

Modern BMS solutions support open standards and Internet-based protocols for seamless interoperability.
