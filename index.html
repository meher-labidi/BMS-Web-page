What is a Building Management System?

A Building Management System (BMS) is a centralized, computer‑based platform that monitors, controls, and optimizes a building's mechanical and electrical systems. It enables real‑time management of HVAC, refrigeration, lighting, power, fire safety, and security systems, ensuring efficient, reliable, and sustainable building operation.

Hardware + software integration – users retrieve data and regulate from a single control point.

Modern BMS solutions support open standards and Internet‑based protocols such as:

BACnetLonWorksModbus DeviceNetSOAPXML
